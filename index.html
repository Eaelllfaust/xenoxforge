FREQUENTLY
ASKED QUESTIONS

Why do you have a subscription based service?

One word - To save you a ton of money.
The cost of hiring an agency like ours or a full time senior-level developer/designer goes up to thousands of dollars. With our subscription, you only get to pay for as long as you’d require our service, and make as many request as you want.
Not to mention you can pause your subscription and continue whenever you want. There couldn’t be a better deal.

Whats the difference between regular requests and your subscription.

If you see your business needing our different services from time to time E.g You require a website from us, then afterwards will be needing graphic illustrations for your website and social accounts for your posts every week. If that’s you, the subscription service would be suitable for you.
While if you just require a job done, then a regular project commitment is perfect.

How soon will my projects be started?

You can expect your project to be started under 24 hours just after payment, and first turnout of requests on average done in 1-3 days.

What if I’m not satisfied with the project

We revise your project until you are satisfied with it. Nothing less.
